Stir fired Basil

Traditional Thai dish that granny like to make in my younger years and still manage to be one of my best dish today.

Let begin

Ingredient

  • Ohly basil
  • Meat of choice
  • Green bean
  • Garlic
  • Osters sauce
  • Fish sauce

Directions

  1. Dicing up the green beans and garlicinto dice shape.
  2. Pre heat the pan with cooking oil of choice medium heat.
  3. Add garlic once pan is pre heated follows with miced meat of choice
  4. Add 50ml of water 2 table spoon of oster sauce, 1 table spoon of fish sauce
  5. Stir untill meat is semi cooked add in green beans and basil
  6. Add more water if the meat became too dry Stir untill you smell the basil

Your done
